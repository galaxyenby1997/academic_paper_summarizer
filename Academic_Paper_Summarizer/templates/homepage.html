A Summarizer for Research Papers!


Are you a college student, graduate student, or a staff
member student cold-emailing professors,
struggling to get the gist of their research papers
so you can walk into their office hours, confidently discuss
their research with them, and ask for a research opportunity?


Ever wondered how you're going to summarize that
20-page research paper in just a paragraph?


Don't fret! On this site, all you'll need to do is click on the
"Summarize" tab on the top of the page, and then enter the website
of the paper into the box that says "HTML page",
enter in how many words you want the summary to be
in the box labeled "Word Count", and this site will do the heavy-lifting for you!
This website will also produce a list of important keywords
to look out for in the article!


For now, this site only accepts full-length papers
that are available as HTML format on a publication site,
or Google Scholars.
